Lesson 1: Cyber Basics

Introduction

Welcome to Cyber Basics! In this lesson, you’ll learn about common online threats, safe browsing, and the foundations of cybersecurity.

Key Concepts

  • Cybersecurity: Protecting devices, networks, and data from attacks.
  • Threat: Anything that can harm your data or privacy.
  • Safe Practices: Using strong passwords, updating devices, and avoiding scams.

Examples

A hacker sends a fake email pretending to be your bank. This is called phishing. If you click the link and enter your password, your account could be stolen.

Best Practices

  • Never share your password with others.
  • Keep your software and apps updated.
  • Be cautious with emails or links from strangers.
