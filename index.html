CyberShield

Our Vision

To empower students with the knowledge, awareness, and resilience needed to thrive in a digitally connected world by fostering a culture of cybersecurity and responsible online behavior.

Our Mission

CyberShield aims to enhance the cybersecurity literacy of Antipolo City Senior High School students by providing an interactive, gamified, and research-based platform. Through engaging lessons, quizzes, and real-world scenarios, we strive to:

  • Strengthen students’ awareness of common cyber threats.

  • Build resilience against phishing, social engineering, and malicious attacks.

  • Promote responsible and secure digital practices in academic and personal life.

  • Support teachers and institutions with a validated tool for cybersecurity education.

Our Objectives

The CyberShield research project is designed to strengthen cybersecurity awareness among Antipolo City Senior High School students. Specifically, it aims to:

  • Assess the current level of cybersecurity awareness of senior high school students.

  • Develop an interactive and gamified learning platform focused on cybersecurity education.

  • Enhance students’ knowledge and skills on identifying and preventing common cyber threats.

  • Evaluate the effectiveness of the CyberShield website as a cybersecurity learning tool.

About CyberShield

CyberShield is a web-based platform developed to strengthen cybersecurity awareness among Antipolo City Senior High School students. It integrates interactive lessons, gamified quizzes, and real-world simulations to make cybersecurity education engaging and effective.

This project is not only aimed at students but also supports teachers and academic institutions by providing a validated tool for improving digital literacy and responsible online practices.

  • Gamified lessons encourage active learning and retention.

  • Helps evaluate the effectiveness of cybersecurity education.

  • Supports schools in fostering a safer digital environment.
